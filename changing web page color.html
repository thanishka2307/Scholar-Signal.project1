How to use Scholar Signal

Everything you need to know — right here.

1

Add your tasks

Go to Tasks and add assignments, exams, or projects. Set a priority (High / Medium / Low) and a due date — Scholar Signal handles the rest.

2

Watch the countdown

Every task shows a live timer. Red = due very soon, yellow = coming up, green = you've got time.

3

Earn chocolate rewards

Every time you mark a task done, you unlock a chocolate treat — Lindor, Kit Kat, Five Star and more. The more you finish, the more you collect!

4

Take a proper break

Burnt out? Head to the Break tab — it'll suggest a fun 5-minute activity so your brain can reset before diving back in.

5

Save everything in Nook

Drop your lecture slides, YouTube links, and study resources into Nook. Your personal library — always one click away.
